Our Story

Meet the team behind Vision AR

We're a diverse team of engineers, designers, and visionaries working to revolutionize how people interact with digital content.

Our Mission

At Vision AR, we believe in a future where technology enhances human capabilities without getting in the way. Our mission is to create intuitive augmented reality experiences that seamlessly blend with everyday life.

Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small team of three engineers to a global company with offices in San Francisco, Tokyo, and Berlin.

Our Values

  • Human-Centered Design

    We create technology that adapts to people, not the other way around.

  • Technological Excellence

    We push the boundaries of what's possible through rigorous engineering and innovation.

  • Privacy by Design

    We build with privacy as a foundation, not an afterthought.
